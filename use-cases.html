Daily Operations Summary

Our AI agent automatically consolidates key business activities from your CRM, project management tools, and email inbox into a concise morning briefing and end-of-day summary.

The system identifies urgent matters, highlights completed tasks, and provides actionable insights on daily operations without requiring a dedicated operations manager.

Save 5+ hours weekly Improved team alignment Reduced oversight

Result: A local marketing agency reduced missed deadlines by 63% and improved client satisfaction scores after implementing this solution.

Inbox Triage Assistant

Our AI assistant checks your email hourly, flags urgent messages, drafts responses to common inquiries, and categorizes emails by topic and priority.

The system can automatically add flagged items to your task list or Slack channel and provide instant notifications for time-sensitive matters.

Reclaim 2 hours daily 50% faster response times Reduced context switching

Result: A solo consultant increased their billable hours by 15% while maintaining the same level of client communication.

Meeting Prep Agent

Two hours before any scheduled meeting, our AI agent creates a comprehensive briefing package that includes the agenda, recent communications with participants, and relevant CRM data.

For client meetings, the system also highlights previous pain points, opportunities, and talking points to ensure you're always prepared.

Higher meeting ROI Enhanced preparation Eliminates last-minute scrambling

Result: A small B2B services company increased their meeting-to-sale conversion rate by 28% after implementing this solution.

Weekly Metrics & KPI Checker

Every Friday, our AI aggregates your critical business metrics like website traffic, sales figures, support tickets, and customer churn into a comprehensive dashboard with week-over-week trends.

The system includes AI-generated insights that explain anomalies and suggest potential actions based on the data patterns it identifies.

Data-driven decisions Early problem detection No manual data gathering

Result: An e-commerce store identified and fixed a checkout issue that was causing a 7% conversion drop before it significantly impacted monthly revenue.

Lead Resurrection Campaign

Our AI system identifies dormant leads in your CRM and automatically launches personalized multi-channel outreach campaigns through email, SMS, and social media.

Using contextual information from previous interactions, the AI creates tailored messages that reference specific pain points and solutions, making cold leads warm again.

Recovered revenue Higher lead value Zero manual follow-up

Result: A real estate agency revived 22% of their stale leads, resulting in three additional sales in the first month of implementation.

CRM Hygiene Assistant

Our AI reviews your CRM weekly to identify data quality issues like missing contact information, duplicate records, and outdated company details.

The system automatically enriches contact data where possible and provides a prioritized list of cleanup actions for your team, ensuring your CRM remains a valuable business asset.

Improved data quality Higher marketing ROI Automated maintenance

Result: A small marketing firm increased their email deliverability by 14% and reduced bounces by 23% after implementing automated CRM hygiene.
